Central Air Conditioning

Central AC remains the most popular choice for whole-home cooling in the Pittsburgh area. Today's systems offer quiet operation, precise temperature control, and significantly improved energy efficiency compared to older units. We install, repair, and maintain central air conditioning systems from leading manufacturers to keep your entire home comfortable.

Ductless Air Conditioning

Ductless mini-split systems deliver powerful cooling without the need for traditional ductwork. These versatile units are an excellent choice for home additions, finished basements, sunrooms, and older homes without existing duct systems. With individual zone control, you can cool specific rooms as needed, reducing energy waste.

Schedule Service

Heat Pumps

Heat pumps provide both cooling and heating in a single system, making them one of the most energy-efficient options available. During the summer, a heat pump works just like an air conditioner, removing heat from your home. In the winter, it reverses the process to provide warmth. This dual functionality can simplify your HVAC setup and lower your year-round energy costs.
